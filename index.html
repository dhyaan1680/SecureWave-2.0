What we do

SecureWave crafts hardened, localized security systems that combine multi-modal sensing, resilient networking, and a compact management gateway. Our solutions are built for places that cannot rely on continuous cloud connectivity — data centers, sensitive facilities, and secure campuses. We design modular sensor nodes, redundant communications, and a robust operator suite to deliver trustworthy presence detection, environmental anomaly alerts, and tamper-resistant monitoring.

Our Product Pillars

Resilient Sensing

Multi-modal sensors with edge processing to detect presence, vibration, and environmental anomalies without cloud dependency.

Redundant Network

Primary wired backbone with wireless fallback to ensure continuous communication even in degraded environments.

Local Gateway

On-premises control panel with role-based access, event timeline, and secure local UI for operators.
