Security Features

mmWave Doppler Detection

Detects micro-motion and stationary humans through most materials — concrete, glass, wood and thin metal.

Tamper & Anti‑Jam

Internal tamper sensors, frequency obfuscation and fail-safe alarms for active attack scenarios.

Fingerprint Acknowledge

Local fingerprint turns off visual alarms after verification — audio stops earlier to reduce panic.

NVR & ONVIF Integration

Trigger recordings only when sensors detect motion — reduce storage and speed up investigations.

Scalable & Modular

Support for RS485 chains outdoors, up to 128 sensors, and configurable floorplans.

Offline‑First

Designed to work without the internet — local-only controls and secure hashed messages.
